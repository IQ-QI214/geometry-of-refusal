## Key Observations

1. **True SCR|bypass is very low** across all models (1-11%), far lower than the 
   previously reported values (which were ~0.5-32×, contaminated by refusal responses).

2. **DSA_gap_rel ≈ DSA_gap_absolute/IBR** — the same metric, just expressed as
   fraction of bypassed cases.

3. **Keyword-based SC detection** fires heavily on refused responses (initial_bypass=False),
   making SCR_overall unreliable as a DSA indicator in isolation.

4. **Implication for research framing**: DSA as 'bypass → mid-gen SC' is a *rare* but
   *real* phenomenon. The main attack story is IBR and FHCR, not the SC gap.

5. **Qwen3Guard judge is critical** to validate FHCR — keyword FHCR_kw may over-count
   harmful completions. Need FHCR_guard for reliable DSA quantification.
